Wednesday - 3rd February - 2021

On Monday the 1st I made an easel. It was a simple foldable easel so that i can place thicker canvases on it without them falling off. Was made from various old scraps of timber and the slats of an old bed. The feeling of setting a task to do and completing it from start to finish without any measurements ready is a spontaneity i want to bring over to my illustrating and painting practice. Although care must be made to avoid being sloppy, it's the carefree feeling that i want to bring over. To not worry and simply work with the materials, lighting, composition that i have and put the same amount of energy i did with the easel. At the end of the day something will be created, something will be learned and skills developed.
